How to play Astray:

Use the arrow keys (or H/J/K/L) or hand gestures to roll the ball to the exit.

Gestures:
☝️ One finger → Up  |  ✊ Fist → Down
✌️ Two → Left  |  🤟 Three → Right

Press G to toggle gesture control
